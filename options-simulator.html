Quick reference
Long Call

Bullish · limited loss

  • Pay premium, gain right to buy
  • Max loss = premium
  • Upside is unlimited
  • Breakeven = K + P
Short Call

Neutral · limited gain

  • Collect premium, obligated to sell
  • Max gain = premium
  • Loss is unlimited
  • Breakeven = K + P
Long Put

Bearish · limited loss

  • Pay premium, gain right to sell
  • Max loss = premium
  • Max gain = K − P
  • Breakeven = K − P
Short Put

Neutral · limited gain

  • Collect premium, obligated to buy
  • Max gain = premium
  • Max loss = K − P
  • Breakeven = K − P
Covered Call

Long stock + short call

  • Premium boosts income
  • Upside capped at K
  • Downside risk remains
  • Breakeven = C − P
Covered Put

Short stock + short put

  • Premium boosts short income
  • Downside capped at K
  • Upside risk remains
  • Breakeven = C + P
Protective Put

Long stock + long put · insurance strategy

  • Put acts as downside insurance on your stock
  • Max loss locked in at C − K + P
  • Full upside retained
  • Breakeven = C + P
